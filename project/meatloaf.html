Meatloaf

I love this meatloaf recipe because it's simple, hearty, and full of flavor. It's one of those classic comfort foods that always reminds me of home-cooked family dinners. Plus, the leftovers make great sandwiches the next day!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 egg
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 1 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 2 tablespoons ketchup
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 cup ketchup and 1 tablespoon brown sugar for topping

Instructions

  1. Set your oven to 350°F (175°C). Lightly grease a loaf pan or line it with parchment paper.
  2. In a large bowl, combine ground beef, egg, onion, breadcrumbs, milk, ketchup, Worcestershire sauce, salt, and pepper. Mix until well combined.
  3. Press the mixture evenly into the loaf pan.
  4. Spread ketchup over the top, and if desired, sprinkle or mix in brown sugar for a sweeter glaze.
  5. Bake for 55060 minutes, or until the internal temperature reaches 160°F (70°C). Let rest for 5-10 minutes before slicing.
