Fried Rice

I love this fried rice recipe because it's quick, filling, and a great way to use up leftover rice and veggies. It's one of those meals that tastes great every time, and you can easily change it up with whatever ingredients you have on hand. Plus, it's ready in just minutes, perfect for busy days!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice (best if chilled, and day old)
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 2 eggs, lightly beaten
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 2 green onions, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium heat. Pour in the beaten eggs and scramble until just set. Remove from the pan and set aside.
  2. Add the remaining oil to the pan. Stir-fry the mixed vegetables for 2-3 minutes until tender.
  3. Stir in the cooked rice, soy sauce, sesame oil, salt, and pepper. Mix well and cook for another 3-4 minutes, letting the rice heat through and get slightly crispy.
  4. Add the scrambled eggs and chopped green onions. Stir to combine and serve warm.
